Roles of the users:
Admin:
-Controls and monitor all functionality in the application
-Create,add,modify,update and delete both students and Lecturers
-Allocate courses to leacturers
-Can only create,modify,update and delete course program




Lecturer:
-Can only create,modify,update and delete Courses
-Set quiz to his/her assigned course
-Can allocate other lecturers to his assigned course
-Can check list of his course and the amount of students who has taken his/her course
